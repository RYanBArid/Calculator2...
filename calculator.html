Formulas

Physics Formulas:

1. Kinematic Equation: \(s = ut + \frac{1}{2}at^2\)

2. Newton's Second Law: \(F = ma\)

3. Work-Energy Theorem: \(W = \Delta KE\)

Trigonometry Formulas:

1. Sine Law: \(\frac{a}{\sin A} = \frac{b}{\sin B} = \frac{c}{\sin C}\)

2. Cosine Law: \(c^2 = a^2 + b^2 - 2ab\cos C\)

3. Pythagorean Theorem: \(c^2 = a^2 + b^2\)
